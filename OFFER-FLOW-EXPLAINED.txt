ğŸ¯ WHAT IS NOTIK.ME?

notik.me is a TRACKING/REDIRECT SERVICE used by some offer networks.

Think of it like:
- bit.ly for link shortening
- BUT for offer tracking

Example Hungeroffer Link:
https://notik.me/api-redirect?token=eyJpdiI6IllYaTY...&user_id=...

This link:
1. Goes to notik.me first (tracking server)
2. Records the click
3. Redirects to actual offer destination
4. Tracks conversion
5. Reports back to OGads
